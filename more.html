Syd is a 20 year old klankontwerper* based in Arnhem making Dutch hyperpop and developing their art by studying at HKU music and technology. They focus on integrating their queer life experiences and vocals into heartfelt productions, with lyrics about love and grief Syd brings their honest sound.

*'klankontwerper' is a term used in the Netherlands for people whom work with synthesis or electronic music.
