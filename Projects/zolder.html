    zolderkamer

    with co-producer Daan Bergman and visuals from Roman-James Zeph

    Art installation centering interaction for the music and technology department, HKU.

    Connection with audience through a desktop, was inspired by internetculture, to be in your attic and surf the web. This is why we called it "zolder", the dutch translation of attic. Daan and I focussed for 6 weeks on putting togheter this performance and we had so much fun. From the technical skills to actually making the music. We hope and plan to have the performance installation played in venues outside of the HKU.
