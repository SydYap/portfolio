    FLIP FLOP

    FLIP FLOP is a fine-arts graduation project designed by Alma van Woudenberg. We started working on the music simultaniously with the repetitions, so the music was carefully curated with each scene and with the players. FLIP FLOP is an absurd performance theater piece about swimmingpools and sexy queer people.
