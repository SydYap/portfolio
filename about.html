They/he

Syd is a 20 year old singer-songwriter and producer/klankontwerper* based in Arnhem making Dutch hyperpop developing their art at HKU school for music and technology. They focus on integrating queer life experiences and vocals into heartfelt productions, with lyrics about love and grief Syd brings their honest sound.

*klankontwerper is a term used in the Netherlands for people whom work with soundsynthesis or electronic music in context ranging from production to art installations.
